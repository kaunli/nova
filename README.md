# 🛍️ Nova – AI-Powered Ecommerce Platform

> A modern ecommerce concept designed to make online shopping more transparent, intelligent, and user-focused.

---

## 📖 Overview

Nova is a concept ecommerce platform that reimagines the online shopping experience through AI-powered recommendations, transparent pricing, and intuitive user-centered design. Inspired by existing marketplaces, Nova focuses on reducing information overload, increasing trust, and helping users make informed purchasing decisions.

This project demonstrates product strategy, UI/UX design, and front-end development through a fully interactive shopping experience.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🛒 Interactive shopping experience
- 🔍 AI-powered product search & recommendations
- 📊 Product comparison tool
- ⭐ AI-generated review summaries
- 📈 Price history tracking
- 🏅 Seller Trust Scores
- ❤️ Wishlist & Favorites
- 🛍️ Shopping cart
- 💳 Streamlined checkout flow
- 📱 Fully responsive design

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

The primary objective of Nova was to design an ecommerce experience that:

- Prioritizes transparency over advertising
- Simplifies product discovery
- Reduces decision fatigue
- Improves trust between buyers and sellers
- Demonstrates modern UX principles
- Creates a polished portfolio-quality product
